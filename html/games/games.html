Wastadium was an attempt at to make an old-school shooter called "TK" made by "ErrorFree", with in-built editor and new make-over. The game is also really easy to mod and add new stuff. It comes with lots of Blender assets which shows how most of the in-game assets were made. I dont own the rights to the music and if the original authors see it as problem, Ill remove them immediately.
